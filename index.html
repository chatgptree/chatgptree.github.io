What If Trees Could Talk?

ChatGPTree turns your trees into interactive guides! A one of a kind platform that brings your trees to life.

Visitors can chat or speak with them, learn about new species, their history, and much more!

Each tree has its own unique personality and quirks to make your conversations unforgettable.

How It Works

1

Scan & Chat

Visitors scan QR codes on trees and instantly start chatting. Each tree comes with a bespoke personality and botanical knowledge - no app download required, just scan and go!

2

Engage & Learn

Trees share educational content through personality quizzes, historical photos, and fascinating botanical facts. Imagine a tree showing you what the city looked like when it was just a sapling!

3

Easy & Customizable

Our lightweight web app is tailored to your needs - an affordable digital art installation for gardens, parks, and community groups. You control the content, personalities, and experience.
